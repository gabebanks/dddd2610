When we think about television, we think about all the things that are great about it—enjoying incredible content with friends and family, being immersed in new worlds, and discovering amazing stories. But we also see where TV could be so much better. The experience of watching TV has been virtually standing still, while innovation has transformed so many other parts of our lives. We think Apple TV is the foundation for the future of television. It’s about making it easier than ever to discover and enjoy your favorite content, and bringing the app experience we all know and love to the biggest screen in your home.

With Apple TV, we've created a platform that brings together the best of what Apple offers—great hardware, software, and services—all in one place. It’s more than just a way to watch your favorite shows and movies; it’s a gateway to apps, games, fitness, and so much more. And with features like Siri, personalized recommendations, and HomeKit integration, Apple TV truly becomes the hub of your connected living room. This is about creating an experience that feels seamless, intuitive, and, most importantly, personal.
